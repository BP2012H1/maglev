The KD-Tree example in examples/persistence/kdtree contains a list of
US Zipcodes (examples/persistence/kdtree/US.txt) from a GeoNames.org

If you use this example dataset, you should also give attribution to GeoNames.
The GeoNames geographical database covers all countries and contains
over eight million placenames that are available for download free of charge.
